Philosophy

The Five major components instrumental in shaping the child are:

  • The Philosophy
  • The Curriculum
  • The Methodology
  • Learning tools
  • Teaching Staff

This philosophy is inspired by MONTESSORI, STEM, MULTI INTELLIGENCE, GURUKUL and KINDERGARTEN.

Every child has an inherent nature and intelligence that is peculiar to him. This varies from child to child and is the most important factor to consider when the child comes to school.

Our Curriculum :

  • Physical
  • Intellectual
  • Language
  • Creative
  • Socio-Emotional

Curriculum is the set of experiences, activities and events, direct or indirect which occur in an environment specially planned to foster children’s learning and development. In simple terms. Curriculum is a plan for learning.
Our Curriculum is a comprehensive child- centric curriculum that comes with set of objectives in all major areas of development. It is related to the child’s development readiness, interests, questions, style of learning and the culture context in which the child finds himself.
