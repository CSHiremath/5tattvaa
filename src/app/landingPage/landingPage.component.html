Philosophy

The Five major components instrumental in shaping the child are:

  • The Philosophy
  • The Curriculum
  • The Methodology
  • Learning tools
  • Teaching Staff

This philosophy is inspired by MONTESSORI, STEM, MULTI INTELLIGENCE, GURUKUL and KINDERGARTEN.

Every child has an inherent nature and intelligence that is peculiar to him. This varies from child to child and is the most important factor to consider when the child comes to school.

Our Curriculum :

Physical

Intellectual

Language

Creative

Socio-Emotional

Curriculum is the set of experiences, activities and events, direct or indirect which occur in an environment specially planned to foster children’s learning and development. In simple terms. Curriculum is a plan for learning.
Our Curriculum is a comprehensive child- centric curriculum that comes with set of objectives in all major areas of development. It is related to the child’s development readiness, interests, questions, style of learning and the culture context in which the child finds himself.

Why Us?

With the number of preschools around you, how would you choose the right one for your child?

Here are some questions that may help you make a better choice.

  • What difference can quality early education make to a child's learning?
  • Is early learning all about learning through fun, music, art, fantasy or is there more?
  • Why does research say that learning stops at 7 and application starts from then on?
  • What is the right age to send your child to a preschool?
  • Is it better to send your child to a regular K-12 school or an Early Education Centre?
  • Do children learn best individually, in small groups or large ones?
  • Will your child be able to transition well into class 1 in a regular school?
  • Does technology hurt or help the learning of a young child?
  • What is the meaning of curriculum in a preschool?
  • What is Montessori, kindergarten, playway, multiple- intelligence etc.?
